High standards

Our crtieria are pretty strict during 2012, we're looking for > +3 sd, which means IQ 145+!

But after 2012, when our expenses for generating data fall a bit, we expect that our criteria will reform to ca. +2.3 sd (IQ 135). So you're still welcome to sign up if you do not at first qualify.
